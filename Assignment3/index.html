Part 2 - Motivation,Method

  • Data Set Description and Preparation
    • Data set was obtained from National Aeronautics and Space Administration
    • Data set from Kobenhabn stations
    • Txt format is not suitable for D3, therefore we simply converted it into CSV format
    • We decided that is would be most suitable to look at the average temperatures for the line chart, as it wouldn't make any sense to display all of the data, which is 12*138 = 1656 data points
    • For the bar chart we chose to look at the monthly data from Apil
  • Result, Discussion
    • The line chart greatly shows that the average temperature over the 138 years only varies about 4 degrees (6,5 at the lowest to 10,5 at the highest)
    • The average temperature in April varies a lot more (3-11 degrees)
    • The everage yearly temperature shows that the climate change might be true
  • Conclusions
    • Chosen diagramms are more or less suitable
    • Climate change is visible
    • Very good dataset, very few missing values, which are clearly indicated
    • An interactive visualiazation might be even more exciting
